## Competition Analysis

### Direct Competitors

1. **EasyPost** ($0.05/label + API fees)
   - Our advantage: Free for rates, one-time purchase
2. **ShipStation** ($9-$159/month)
   - Our advantage: Lower cost, self-hosted
3. **Shippo** (Similar to EasyPost)
   - Our advantage: No per-transaction fees

### Market Gap

- **No modern PHP USPS-only library** with OAuth 2.0
- Most solutions are multi-carrier (bloated, expensive)
- Developers want simple, free, open source
